ADHD Features

Inattention

  • Struggling to stay focused on tasks
  • Making frequent mistakes due to carelessness
  • Finding it hard to stay organised
  • Easily distracted by thoughts or surroundings
  • Forgetting everyday tasks or appointments
  • Avoiding tasks requiring sustained concentration
  • Difficulty finishing projects

Hyperactivity

  • Feeling restless or constantly “on the go”
  • Fidgeting, tapping, or moving frequently
  • Talking excessively
  • Difficulty engaging in calm activities
  • Preferring fast-paced environments

Impulsivity

  • Acting quickly without thinking
  • Difficulty waiting your turn
  • Interrupting conversations
  • Blurting out thoughts
  • Impatience in daily situations
